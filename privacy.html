Privacy Policy

How we handle your information.

This is placeholder content for demonstration purposes. Final policy should be reviewed before publishing.

1. Information we collect

When you contact Lineside Electrical Limited through our website, by phone or by email, we may collect your name, contact details, address and details about the work you need done. We only collect what we need to quote and carry out the job.

2. How we use it

We use your information to respond to enquiries, prepare quotes, schedule and complete work, issue invoices, and keep in touch about your job. We don't sell your information to anyone.

3. Sharing

We may share limited information with suppliers or contractors where it's needed to complete your job, and with our accountant or as required by law. Otherwise your details stay with us.

4. Form submissions

Enquiry and feedback forms on this site are delivered to us by email via a third-party form service. Information you submit is used only to respond to your enquiry.

5. Storage & security

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure and to retain it only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law.

6. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you can ask to see the personal information we hold about you and request a correction. Get in touch and we'll help.

7. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email joe@lineside-electrical.co.nz or call 021 415 646.
