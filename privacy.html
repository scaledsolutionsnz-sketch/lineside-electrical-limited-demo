Privacy Policy

Lineside Electrical Limited respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle the information you share with us, in line with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

What we collect

When you contact us through this website we collect only what you provide — typically your name, phone number, email, suburb, and a description of the work you need. We don't ask for anything we don't need to quote and complete your job.

How we use it

We use your details to respond to your enquiry, prepare a quote, schedule work, and stay in touch about your job. We do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

Sharing

Your information stays with Lineside Electrical. We only share it where it's needed to complete your job (for example a supplier or sub-trade) or where the law requires it.

Storage and security

Enquiry details submitted through this site are delivered to us by email through a third-party form service. We keep your information only as long as needed for your job and our records.

Your choices

You can ask to see the information we hold about you, correct it, or have it removed. Just get in touch and we'll sort it.
