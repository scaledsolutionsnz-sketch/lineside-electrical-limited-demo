What we do

Electrical work for homes, rentals and small commercial.

From a single power point to a full rewire — if it runs on electricity, Lineside can sort it. Here's the work we get called for most around Christchurch.

Switchboard upgrades

Old fuse boards and tripping circuits replaced with a modern board, RCDs and surge protection — safer power for the whole house.

Lighting & LED

Downlights, sensor lights, outdoor and security lighting, and swapping tired fittings for efficient LED that cuts your power bill.

Power points & wiring

New outlets, USB points, outdoor sockets and extra circuits — added neatly where you actually need them, without the guesswork.

Fault finding & repairs

Power keeps cutting out or something's not working? We track down the fault, explain what's wrong, and fix it properly.

Heat pumps & EV chargers

Wiring and dedicated circuits for heat pumps, hot water, ventilation and home EV charger installs — done to spec.

Renovations & rewires

Planning a reno or buying an older home? We rewire, relocate and pre-wire so the electrical is sorted before the linings go on.
