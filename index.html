What we do

Electrical services for Christchurch homes & trades

01

Rewires & New Wiring

Full and partial rewires for older homes, plus wiring for extensions, sleepouts and new builds.

02

Switchboard Upgrades

Swap a tired fuse box for a modern switchboard with RCD protection so power stays safe and reliable.

About Lineside

Straight-up electrical work from a local team

Lineside Electrical is a Christchurch-based electrical business built on doing the job properly and treating people's homes like our own. From a single dodgy power point to wiring a whole new build, every job gets the same care.

You'll deal with the same people start to finish — no call centres, no being passed around. We explain what needs doing in plain language, quote it up front, and leave the place tidy.

  • Locally owned & operated in Christchurch
  • Up-front quotes — no surprise invoices
  • Safe, tidy, code-compliant workmanship
